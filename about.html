About Us

Our Story - Kusum Innovations

Founded on 29th June 2016, Kusum Innovations is a trusted name in developing websites and mobile apps, digital marketing, and video production in Delhi-NCR. In a fast-paced industry where trends often overshadow creativity, we focus on delivering impactful strategies and visually compelling content.

Over the years, we’ve had the privilege of working with a diverse range of clients—startups, established brands, and enterprises—helping them bring their ideas to life through innovative digital solutions.

With a potent blend of creativity, technology, and strategic thinking, we craft experiences that not only look great but also drive real results. From designing intuitive websites and mobile apps to creating engaging ad campaigns and powerful visual stories, we are dedicated to helping brands connect, grow, and leave a lasting impression.

Mission

At Kusum Innovations, our mission is driven by ambition, creativity, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. We’re on a journey to become the best advertising agency and best video production company in the industry. For us, it’s not just about delivering services — it’s about creating experiences that inspire, engage, and make a real difference for our clients.

We strive to offer a powerful mix of digital marketing, video production, and website and mobile app development — all tailored to fuel our clients’ growth and visibility. Every project we take on is guided by innovation, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of our client’s vision.

Our team is our biggest asset — a dynamic group of professionals who bring passion, skill, and fresh ideas to the table. Together, we are committed to turning bold dreams into reality and helping businesses thrive in an ever-evolving digital world, solidifying our position as the best advertising agency and best video production company in Delhi-NCR and beyond.

Vision

Kusum Innovations is here to shake up the advertising industry with a bold, fresh perspective. We aim to redefine the norms and inject new energy into this fast-evolving space. With a team of passionate and creative advertising professionals, we’re ready to help our clients rise above the noise, dominate their markets, and become the absolute best in their game.

For us, true success lies in the smiles we bring and the milestones we help achieve. We’re not just here to offer services — we’re here to deliver results. From cutting-edge marketing strategies to impactful video content and the latest digital solutions, we’re all in when it comes to driving growth and accelerating success.

We believe that everyone needs a strong partner to reach extraordinary heights — and that’s where Kusum Innovations comes in. We’re the spark that adds the extra to your ordinary.

Core Values

Kusum Innovations strives to become the Number One Advertising Firm. Success doesn’t come overnight — it’s built on dedication, vision, and strong core values. From day one, we committed to four key values that continue to guide everything we do.

Self-definition: Our motive is clear. We are self-aware of the duties we wish to undertake. We have come into existence because we want to provide diverse technology-driven services
to our clients, for their benefit and success.

Transparency: We don’t believe in barriers. We maintain a transparent relationship with our clients, where they have absolute authority to question us, criticise us and even
discuss alternative methods of their liking.

Authenticity: We wish to bring some freshness to the industry methods in our own style. We promise to introduce this revolution with our unique solutions incorporating the best of the
latest trends, with a hint of newness guaranteed.

Accountability: We hardly ever miss the bull’s eye, but even if we do - we accept our shortcomings with humility. Customer satisfaction is above all and our job is complete only when the customer gives us all thumbs up!
